Welcome to timbuktu!
====================================

What is in a name?
------------------
The name ``timbuktu`` was chosen for its historical significance in the preservation of knowledge and being a world centre of learning.

What is the purpose?
--------------------
``timbuktu`` aims to be a Compendium, Encyclopaedia, Guide, Index, or whatever you would like to call it for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) in Africa.
